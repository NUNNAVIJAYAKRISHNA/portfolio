Selected Work

campus connect

Colleges often struggle with effective event management and publicity, as traditional methods like manual announcements and scattered social media updates lead to poor student awareness and low participation. To address this issue, we propose CampusConnect—a centralized web platform that streamlines communication and enhances student engagement within AITAM College. The platform aims to serve as a one-stop solution for students to stay informed about upcoming events, receive instant updates, and actively participate. For organizers, it reduces workload by simplifying outreach and improving overall attendance. By bridging communication gaps, CampusConnect aims to create a more connected and interactive campus culture.

Smart Syllabus Coverage Tracker

In many colleges, monitoring syllabus progress remains inefficient, as faculty and students lack a real-time platform to track completion. Students often struggle to identify whether they have understood specific topics, and teachers face difficulty in addressing weak areas effectively. This leads to gaps in preparation and poor exam performance. To address this, the project proposes a Smart Syllabus Coverage Tracker, a web-based system where faculty can upload daily topic completion, mark important areas, and share additional resources. Students can mark their understanding levels, and faculty can provide helpful links for difficult topics, ensuring personalized support. The tracker also benefits HODs and administrators, as they can monitor the overall progress of syllabus coverage across different subjects and ensure timely completion.
